📖 What is this?

Welcome to PaperTrade — practice the markets without the risk.

PaperTrade is a playful, social way to learn how trading works. You start with virtual cash, buy real-world stocks & crypto at live prices, collect items, mine for treasure, and trade with other players — all without putting a real dollar on the line. Mess up? Try again tomorrow. 🚀

How it works in 5 steps

1

Sign up & get free starter cash

Every new account gets virtual money to play with. No deposits, no card details — it's pretend money for practice. 💸

2

Pick a stock or coin to buy

Head to the Market tab, search for something you like (Apple, Tesla, Bitcoin, …) and tap Buy. Prices update live. 📊

3

Watch your Portfolio

Your Portfolio tab shows everything you own and how much you've gained or lost. Green is good, red means it's sale time. 📈

4

Sell when you're ready

Tap any holding and hit Sell. The cash goes back into your wallet so you can try a new pick. No fees panic — small spread only. 💰

5

Have fun with the rest 🎮

Open packs in the Shop, swap items with friends, mine for tokens, post to Reels, and climb the leaderboards. It's a market and a hangout.

Your Wallet, explained

Your wallet holds two things. Think of it like having dollars in one pocket and arcade tokens in the other.

Pocket 1
💵 Virtual Cash
Used to buy stocks, crypto and items. You earn it by selling things, completing activities or topping up via Cash In.
Pocket 2
🪙 Tokens
A bonus currency from mining and special drops. Convert tokens to cash any time from the Wallet tab — no penalty.

What's a Portfolio?

A portfolio is just a fancy word for everything you currently own. When you buy 1 share of Apple, it shows up in your portfolio with the price you paid. As Apple's price moves, your portfolio shows the profit or loss in real time. Sell, and the money returns to your wallet. That's it — no hidden complexity. 🎯

Quick questions

Is this real money?

Nope! Every dollar in PaperTrade is virtual. You can't lose real cash, and you can't withdraw real cash either. It's a sandbox to practice and have fun.

Where do the prices come from?

Stocks and crypto pull from live market data, so what you see is close to what real traders see. Items and tokens are priced by other players in the Shop and Swap tabs.

I ran out of cash. Now what?

Sell something! Open your Portfolio, tap a holding, and hit Sell. Or visit Cash In to convert your tokens. You can also mine for new tokens.

How does Swap differ from the Shop?

The Shop is a public list of things for sale at a fixed price. Swap is a back-and-forth trade with one specific person — both sides add what they're offering, and both have to confirm.

Can I message other players?

Yes — open Messages, search a username, and start chatting. You can even gift cash or items right inside the conversation.

What are Reels?

A short-video feed for the community. Post your big wins, ask questions, share memes — same idea as the swipe-up videos you already know.
